# UI/UX Layout Design

## Public Pages
- **Homepage**
  - Hero section with featured products
  - Navigation bar with links to product categories
  - Footer with contact info, links to policies and social media

- **Product Listings**
  - Grid layout for products with filters (price, category, rating)
  - Sort options (newest, price low to high, etc.)

- **Product Details**
  - Product images with zoom functionality
  - Detailed description, specifications, and reviews
  - Add to cart button and related products section

- **Login/Registration**
  - Simple forms for user authentication
  - Options for social media login

- **Contact Us**
  - Simple form to submit inquiries
  - Map integration for physical store locations

## Vendor Dashboard
- **Overview**
  - Summary of sales, active listings, and pending orders
  - Notifications for messages and new orders

- **Product Management**
  - Interface to add/edit product listings
  - Analytics for product performance

- **Order Management**
  - View and manage incoming orders
  - Facilities for order fulfillment and tracking

- **Account Settings**
  - Profile management and payment settings

## Buyer Dashboard
- **Overview**
  - Summary of recent orders and favorite products
  - Notifications for order updates and promotions

- **Order History**
  - Detailed view of past purchases
  - Option to reorder items

- **Profile Settings**
  - Manage shipping addresses and payment methods

## Checkout Flow
- **Cart Summary**
  - List of items to be purchased with quantity and price
  - Options to apply discount codes and estimate shipping

- **Shipping Information**
  - Form to enter or select shipping address

- **Payment Information**
  - Secure form for payment details (credit card, PayPal, etc.)

- **Review Order**
  - Final review of items, shipping, and payment
  - Confirmation button to complete the purchase

- **Confirmation Page**
  - Thank you message and order summary
  - Links for tracking the order status and support options.
